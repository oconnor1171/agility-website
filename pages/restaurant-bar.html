Restaurant & Bar Services

Specialized Accounting for Food Service Industry

Our Restaurant Services

P&L Statements

Comprehensive Profit and Loss analysis tailored to the restaurant industry. We provide clear, actionable reporting that shows you exactly where your money is coming from and where it's going. Our detailed P&L statements include key metrics specific to food service operations.

Benchmark Ratio Analysis

Compare your restaurant's financial metrics against industry benchmarks. Our ratio analysis helps you identify areas of strength and opportunities for improvement. We analyze food cost percentages, labor ratios, and other critical metrics to help you optimize profitability.

Cash Flow Management

Manage your restaurant's cash flow effectively with our specialized cash flow analysis and forecasting. Understanding when money comes in and goes out is critical in the food service industry. We help you plan for seasonal fluctuations and ensure adequate working capital.

Sales Tax Reporting

Stay compliant with all sales tax reporting requirements. From daily reconciliation to monthly filings, we handle the complexities of sales tax in the restaurant industry. Our systems ensure accurate reporting and compliance with state and local regulations.

COGS Analysis (Cost of Goods Sold)

Control your food costs with our detailed COGS analysis. We help you track inventory, identify waste, and optimize purchasing to maximize your gross profit. Understanding your cost of goods is essential for restaurant profitability.

Tip Reporting & Payroll Compliance

Navigate the complexities of tip reporting and payroll in restaurants. We ensure accurate tip tracking, compliance with federal and state tip requirements, and proper payroll withholding. Our team stays current with all regulatory changes affecting restaurant payroll.
