Real Estate Services

Expert Accounting & Tax Services for Real Estate Professionals

Our Real Estate Services

General Ledger Management

Proper general ledger management is the foundation of accurate real estate accounting. We set up and maintain comprehensive general ledger systems for all your properties. Our organized structure makes it easy to track income, expenses, and profitability by property and by category.

Cash Flow Management

Real estate involves managing rents, tenant deposits, expenses, and capital improvements. We provide detailed cash flow analysis and forecasting to ensure you have adequate liquidity for all your needs. We help you understand when cash comes in and goes out, and plan for seasonal variations.

Accounts Payable & Accounts Receivable

Track all your property-related payables and receivables. From tenant rents to vendor invoices, we maintain clear records of what you owe and what is owed to you. Our AP/AR management ensures nothing falls through the cracks.

Budgeting & Forecasting

Plan for the future with detailed budgets and forecasts for your real estate portfolio. We help you project rental income, anticipate maintenance and repair expenses, plan for capital improvements, and forecast profitability. Good planning leads to better decision-making.

Property Financial Reporting

Get clear, detailed financial reports for each property in your portfolio. Our property-specific reporting shows you exactly how each property is performing. Understand your income, expenses, profit margins, and cash flow for every property you own.

Tax Planning & Compliance

Maximize deductions and minimize taxes for your real estate portfolio. We optimize depreciation schedules, plan cost segregation studies, and ensure compliance with all tax reporting requirements. Strategic tax planning can save real estate investors thousands of dollars.

Optimize Your Real Estate Portfolio

Let's review your real estate financial performance and identify opportunities to improve profitability and reduce taxes.
